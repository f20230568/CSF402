Force Directed Layout (Eades):


Algorithm: Spring-Embedder

Based on Peter Eades (1984), the implementation uses a physics model:

1. Coulomb's Law (Repulsion)
Force = repulsion / distance²

Applied to all pairs of nodes. It pushes vertices apart to ensure space.

2. Hooke's Law (Attraction)
Force = attraction * distance

Applied only to connected nodes. It acts like a spring pulling neighbors together.

3. Velocity & Damping
New_Pos = Old_Pos + (Force * damping)

Damping prevents the system from oscillating infinitely by absorbing energy.
